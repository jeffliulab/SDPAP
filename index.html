Automated Production

Automation Technology: Utilizing robots, artificial intelligence, and other advanced technologies to minimize human labor in the production process. Automation can significantly increase production efficiency, reduce costs, and improve product quality.

Productivity Enhancement: Fully automated production can dramatically boost productivity, enabling society to produce a wide range of goods and services more efficiently.

Social Democracy

Economic Equality: Social democracy emphasizes achieving economic equality through government intervention and social policies, ensuring everyone has access to basic life necessities and welfare.

Public Services: Social democratic states typically provide extensive public services, including healthcare, education, housing, and social welfare, to enhance citizens' quality of life.

Democratic Participation: Social democracy values citizens' democratic participation, advocating for the formulation and implementation of public policies through democratic processes.

Integration of Automated Production and Social Democracy

Wealth Distribution: In a society with fully automated production, overall wealth increases due to enhanced productivity. Social democratic policies can ensure this wealth is distributed fairly, reducing the gap between rich and poor.

Work and Income: As automation becomes widespread, traditional jobs decrease, necessitating a redefinition of work and income sources. Social democracy can provide basic income or other forms of social security to ensure everyone has the essentials for living.

Work-Life Balance: Automated production can reduce working hours, allowing people more free time for leisure, learning, and personal development, thereby improving the quality of life.

Progressivism

Progressivism is a social and political movement aimed at achieving societal improvement and advancement through social reforms and innovations. The core principles of progressivism include:

  • Social Justice: Fairness and justice, striving to eliminate inequalities in society, including those related to race, gender, economics, and education.
  • Economic Reform: Promoting economic policy reforms to ensure fair distribution of wealth and resources, reducing the gap between the rich and the poor, and supporting workers' rights and better working conditions.
  • Environmental Protection: Addressing environmental issues, advocating for sustainable development, and pushing for policies to combat climate change and protect natural resources.
  • Technological Innovation: Encouraging technological progress and innovation, supporting scientific research and education.
  • Democratic Participation: Advocating for broad citizen participation and democratic decision-making.
  • Social Welfare: Supporting a robust social security system to improve the quality of life for all members of society.

Automated Production Era

机器人、人工智能将取代廉价劳动力，后发优势、人口红利将不复存在；落后的国家将彻底失去进步的可能性。

人类社会生产关系、劳资关系、劳动力和市场的关系都将被彻底颠覆。

如果私有制仍然存在，那么自动化生产工厂的成果将属于谁？财富分化将进一步扩大。拥有自动化生产能力的人，将在自动化生产的道路上成为垄断者。在分化的初期，SDPAP如果不能抢占意识形态高地，人类社会将滑向赛博朋克时代。届时，拥有超大规模生产能力的公司将成为新时代的东印度公司，在实质上殖民其他缺乏自动化生产能力的国家和地区。

自动化生产是不可避免的，人类对于高生产力的追求是不可逆转的，如果SDPAP不能实现，那么未来的发展轨迹将会是：

  • 机器人拥有不可比拟的优势：生产精准、不知疲倦。随着时间的发展，机器人替代的工种将越来越多，失业人口不断增加。
  • 一旦收回初期成本，机器人们将像无思想能力的奴隶们那样不知疲倦地工作，并让工厂进入持续产能过剩的阶段。
  • 当产能过剩需要倾销商品时，那些没有自动化生产能力的地区将被迫打开市场，当地的民众将陷入贫困。
  • 一旦失业工人、大多数落后地区的人失去劳动的可能性，他们的劳动价值将归零，并最终沦为“玩物”、“仆人”等角色。
  • 人体买卖、奴隶制度将重回人类社会，此时奴隶们将从事服侍性工作而非劳动性工作，因为劳动将由机器人完成；大多数人的劳动价值将仅剩情绪价值和贬低价值，沦为肉体玩具、提供情绪价值的仆人等。
  • 随着生物科技的进一步发展，富人将在后代基因选择上拥有更大的话语权，富人的后代将兼具高智商和好身体，从而进一步扩大贫富差距，让贫富差距从阶级差距上升到物种差距。

然而，如果能在全自动化生产初期就发展SDPAP，那么未来将是：

  • 机器人进行基础农业、工业劳动，产出大量农产品、工业产品，这些产品可以分配给所有的民众；或者通过全民发薪的方式，让经济持续流通，并可以避免产能过剩的问题。
  • 贫困人口的生活水平上升到今天的中间阶层，所有人都能得到基本的生活保障、住房保障。
  • 有闲阶层空前壮大，文娱产业、科学发展加速进步。
  • 随着科学技术的进一步发展，全自动化生产的规模进一步加深，人们的生活水平也一并提升。社会将逐步趋近于一个扁平化的全民皆富裕的状态，人们不再将财富视为优越感的来源，而将智慧、科学研究等视为优越感的来源。新的社会评价体系逐渐诞生，科学公社、科学社团、甚至科学国度将会诞生，绝大多数人都将从事研究性工作而非劳动性工作。
